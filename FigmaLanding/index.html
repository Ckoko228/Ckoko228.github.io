MiSide

Developed by: AIHASTO

Release: 10/12/2024

Features of the Experience

The main part of the gameplay is a first-person adventure inside a simulation. The location of the action is Mita's virtual apartment in different versions of the game, as well as abnormal places and areas outside the boundaries of the "game map". The player explores the virtual world of the mobile game, solves puzzles, and manipulates objects.
